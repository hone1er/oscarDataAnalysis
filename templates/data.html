    INTRODUCTION:

    The Oscars (also confusingly referred to as the Academy Awards) is a big awards ceremony in the eyes of Hollywood. Winning an Oscar is a sign of high status and recognition. How does one achieve such an award and is the academy fully transparent about the path to this esteemed recognition? We are here to find out. In this analysis we will be diving into the various factors that go into being nominated. We will first look at what the Academy states is the way to become nominated and then, as data geeks, we will hunt for the data to back-up or debunk the claims.
